About Legacy Custom Homes

Legacy Custom Homes has been building quality homes throughout the Triangle continuously for over 35 years. Our unique, customer focused, build process is a true partnership with the customer, dedicated to delivering quality homes that realize your unique style and exceed your expectations.

We are honored to have earned a reputation for providing high quality homes at an exceptional value in all price points. Each Legacy Home is carefully constructed by the area's most respected craftsmen and constructed of only top quality materials.

From $800,000 townhomes up to $4 Million luxury homes, we maintain the same attention to detail, diligent documentation, and highest service level possible. This is how Legacy has maintained an impeccable reputation over the years.
